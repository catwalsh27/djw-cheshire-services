About Daniel

Daniel J Walsh is a multi-skilled electrical engineer with 40 years of experience in domestic and commercial electrics, and 15 years in commercial facilities management. Based in South Manchester, Daniel has worked on everything from single-room rewires to large-scale commercial projects for major clients across the UK, Ireland and Europe.

His background covers the full spectrum of electrical engineering — from domestic installations and consumer unit upgrades to three-phase commercial power, fire and security systems, air conditioning, UPS and generator systems, and structured data cabling. When you hire Daniel, you get one of the most qualified and experienced engineers in South Manchester.

Daniel has held senior engineer and supervisor roles throughout his career, including Lead Maintenance Engineer for CBRE/Norland, one of the UK's largest facilities management companies.
