EV Charger Installation

As an OZEV (Office for Zero Emission Vehicles) Approved Installer, Daniel J Walsh Electrical Services is fully qualified to install domestic and workplace electric vehicle charge points. Whether you drive a Tesla, Nissan Leaf, BMW i-series or any other EV, we can recommend and install the right charger to suit your vehicle and your home setup.

We install a wide range of 7kW smart home chargers, assess your existing electrical installation to confirm suitability, handle all cabling from your consumer unit to the charge point, and issue a full electrical completion certificate. Workplace charge point installations may be eligible for funding through government schemes. Ask us for up-to-date information when you get in touch.

Installation typically takes half a day and is carried out neatly with concealed cabling wherever possible.

Rewiring & Testing

An ageing or faulty electrical installation is a serious safety hazard. If your property has not been rewired in the last 25–30 years, or if you have noticed flickering lights, tripping breakers, or discoloured sockets, it may be time for an inspection or a full rewire. Daniel J Walsh Electrical Services carries out full and partial rewires on domestic and commercial properties throughout South Manchester.

We also issue Electrical Installation Condition Reports (EICRs), which are now a legal requirement for all private rented properties in England and must be renewed every five years. An EICR provides a thorough inspection of your existing wiring and installation, identifying any code C1, C2 or C3 defects and giving you the documentation you need for landlord compliance or property sales.

All rewiring work is notified to Building Control under Part P of the Building Regulations and a full completion certificate is provided on finishing. We take great care to minimise plasterwork damage during rewires and can advise on making good after the job is complete.

Rewire prices vary significantly depending on property size and condition. Please get in touch for a free on-site survey and quotation.
